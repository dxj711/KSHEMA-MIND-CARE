About Us

Kshema Mind Care offers holistic treatment for mental health, addiction, and behavioral issues. With psychotherapy, counseling, detox, and relaxation therapy, it focuses on personalized care. Through empowerment activities and recreation, it promotes well-being. Committed to restoring mental health and balance in a peaceful environment.
